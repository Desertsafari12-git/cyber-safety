HOW TO PROTECT PERSONEL INFORMATION

To protect personal data and devices, teens should be careful about what they share online, like personal contact information, photos, and even details in their usernames that could be used to identify them. It's important to use strong, unique passwords for different accounts and to enable multi-factor authentication whenever possible.
